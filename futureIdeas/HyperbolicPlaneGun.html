Hyperbolic Plane Gun

Inspired by: instructions I used to make the model manually and the strife that ensued.

I want to design what is essentially a packing tape dispenser that can be used to create a polygonal hyperbolic plane.

Sort of limited as far as use cases go. In order to make the "gun" you'd replace the roll of tape with a roll of paper and add a mechanism to crease the paper. If we're going for gold, there's a mechanism to tape the strip you've made to existing strips in your model.
