Book of Math Spells

Inspired by:

I've explained my desire for more narrative math pieces in _____. This idea is very much related. I've spent a lot of time reflecting on where my interest in math comes from and I think it's a byproduct of hypervigilance. When faced with decisions or trouble, big or small, I spent a lot of time systemizing the situation at hand. I tried to find structure amongst groups of people especially. To be clear none of this was conscious so much as it was a natural mode of thought for me. Eventually I began to recognize similarities within these "structures" and/or the behavior of people within these "structures." I think this is why learning about groups and morphisms felt validating in a sense. I hope this didn't sound too sociopathic.

Anyway, I think math feels sort of like magic. So that coupled with my bend for seeing math in people makes for a spellbook.
